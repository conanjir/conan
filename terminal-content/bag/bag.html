Bag Contents Data Visualisation

1. Personal Bag Contents

This was an exercise in data visualisation, revealing the contents inside bags. The first image showcases the contents of my own bag, arranged in order of personal importance — from the items I'd be most upset to lose, to those I'd be least sad about.

2. Grouped Bag Contents

The second image displays the combined contents of my bag and those of two classmates. We visualised our items according to how likely they would be banned by the government — from most to least. Alternatively, you could interpret the ordering as a scale from least moral or most harmful to most moral or least harmful.

It’s interesting to observe that the items most likely to cause harm to society tend to be more technically complex — they require more components and effort to produce. Perhaps this suggests a need to return to simpler times and make better use of simple goods. These items, it seems, cause the least harm to both oneself and others.
